How to Play

  1. Objective: Get 3 of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  2. Turns: Players alternate placing X and O on the 3x3 grid
  3. Starting Player: Player X always goes first
  4. Game End: Win when you get 3 in a row, or draw when board is full
